Key Findings
Critical Insights

Derived directly from data. Operational speed outweighs demographic targeting or payment optimization.

01
Delivery Speed = Revenue
5-minute deliveries generate ₹15.3M — 62.8% of total revenue. The drop after 10 minutes is steep and non-linear. SLA compliance is the #1 business KPI.
Critical
02
11× On-Time Revenue Gap
On-time deliveries (≤15 min) generate ₹22M versus just ₹2M from late orders. Eliminating late deliveries could recover up to ₹2M per order cycle.
Critical
03
Ratings Lag at 3.20/5
Average customer satisfaction is below benchmark. Late deliveries are the primary driver. Achieving ≥4.0 is possible through SLA improvements alone.
High Priority
04
Demographics Don't Drive Revenue
Age group, gender, and payment method show minimal variation in revenue. Demographic-targeted campaigns are lower ROI than operational improvements.
Medium
05
Loyal Customers = Higher AOV
Customer segment shows moderate variation. Loyal customers generate higher average order values, validating investment in retention over acquisition.
Medium
06
Grocery & Dairy Lead Categories
Core categories drive consistent, recurring demand. Revenue is evenly distributed, but grocery and dairy show strongest habitual repurchase behavior.
Medium
